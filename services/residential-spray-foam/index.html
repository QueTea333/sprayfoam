Residential Applications

Attic Insulation

The #1 source of energy loss. Spray foam creates a perfect seal in your attic, preventing heat loss in winter and heat gain in summer.

Basement Insulation

Keep your basement warm, dry, and usable year-round. Spray foam prevents moisture intrusion and creates a comfortable living space.

Crawl Space Insulation

Protect your home's foundation and improve overall efficiency by sealing and insulating crawl spaces.

Wall Insulation

Retrofit existing walls or insulate new construction with superior thermal and sound barriers.

Rim Joist Insulation

Seal one of the most overlooked areas of heat loss in your home with targeted spray foam application.

Open Cell vs Closed Cell

Open Cell Spray Foam: Lighter, more affordable option ideal for interior walls and sound dampening. R-value of 3.5-3.7 per inch.

Closed Cell Spray Foam: Denser, higher R-value (R-6 to R-7 per inch), acts as vapor barrier. Perfect for exterior walls, roofs, and areas requiring maximum insulation and structural support.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does residential spray foam cost?

Costs typically range from $1.50-$3.50 per square foot, depending on the type, thickness, and project size. We provide free detailed quotes within 24 hours of inspection.

What is the ROI on spray foam insulation?

Most homeowners see 30-50% reduction in energy bills, with full ROI achieved in 3-7 years. Additionally, spray foam increases home value and comfort immediately.

Is spray foam insulation safe?

Yes, once cured (24-48 hours), spray foam is completely safe, non-toxic, and won't off-gas. It also prevents mold growth and improves indoor air quality.

How long does installation take?

Most residential projects are completed in 1-3 days, depending on the size and complexity. You can typically re-enter your home 24 hours after application.
