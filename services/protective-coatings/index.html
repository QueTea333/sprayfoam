Protect Your Spray Foam Investment

Spray foam insulation is an excellent investment, but exposed foam can degrade from UV exposure, physical damage, and environmental factors. Our protective coatings add an extra layer of protection, ensuring your insulation performs optimally for decades.

Benefits of Protective Coings

  • ✓ UV Protection: Prevents sun damage and degradation
  • ✓ Fire Resistance: Adds fire-retardant properties
  • ✓ Physical Protection: Guards against impacts and abrasion
  • ✓ Moisture Resistance: Additional water protection
  • ✓ Aesthetic Finish: Clean, professional appearance
  • ✓ Extended Lifespan: Maximizes insulation longevity

Coating Applications

Exposed Foam Protection

Ideal for exposed spray foam in attics, crawl spaces, and unfinished areas requiring UV and fire protection.

Commercial Applications

Warehouse ceilings, commercial roofs, and industrial facilities benefit from protective coatings.

Exterior Applications

Protect exterior foam applications from weather, UV, and environmental damage.
