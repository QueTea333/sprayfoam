About Innovated SFC

Southern Alberta's trusted spray foam insulation experts since 2009

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Innovated SFC has grown from a small local operation to Southern Alberta's premier spray foam insulation contractor. Our mission has always been simple: provide the highest quality insulation services with exceptional customer care.

Over the past 15+ years, we've completed more than 500 residential and commercial projects across Southern Alberta and British Columbia. From single-family homes to large commercial warehouses, we bring the same commitment to quality and customer satisfaction to every job.
